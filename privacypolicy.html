DriftGuard Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

1. Information We Collect

DriftGuard is designed to be a privacy-focused tool. We do not collect or store any personal data that identifies you directly. All core application data remains entirely local to your device.

Locally Stored Data

The following information is stored only in your browser's local storage or memory:

  • Uploaded Audio Files: Files uploaded for custom cues are temporarily stored in your browser's memory and are deleted when the session ends or the app is closed. They are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Session Preferences: Your selected focus time, break duration, and reminder interval are saved locally to streamline future use.

2. Third-Party Services (Analytics and Advertising)

We use third-party services to support and analyze our service, including advertising (for which this policy is being prepared).

Google AdSense / Analytics

If advertisements are displayed, they are served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. Please review the Google AdSense Privacy Policy for information on how they handle data collection and tracking.

Links to Other Sites

Our application contains links to external sites (like our Feedback form or Contact Us email). We have no control over the content or privacy practices of these sites and assume no responsibility for them.

3. Data Usage

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not use your personal information for any purpose beyond serving basic non-personalized advertisements (if applicable) and facilitating the core function of the application (e.g., storing your timer preferences locally).

4. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
